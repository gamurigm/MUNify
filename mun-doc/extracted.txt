nuclear security crisis (Fedchenko, 2023; Zurita, 2024).

A. Foundation and Strategic Objectives
To achieve immediate and veriable compliance with nuclear safety standards, the Delegation
calls for the negotiation and adoption of a Protocol of Guaranteed Restraint (PGR)a

The urgency of this proposal stems from the documented deterioration of safety margins at

supply chains, the facility operates dangerously close to conditions that could precipitate
core damage. Only guaranteed cessation of military activities in critical zones can arrest this
degradation and restore the margin of safety required to prevent disaster.
